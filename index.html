Facts about Paris Sait German

With over 30 titles to their name, PSG is the most successful club in football history in France. They have won Ligue 1 six times, the domestic French football league containing teams like Bordeaux, Monaco, Brest, Reims, Marseille, and a few tough competitors. PSG has also won the Trophee des Champions, Coupe de Ligue, and Coupe de France, one of the only two French sides ever to win a European title – the UEFA Cup Winners’ Cup in 1996 and UEFA Intertoto Cup in 2001.
